Haha akhirnya ketemu juga, ini baca ya pesan nya

I know how hard it is to let you go.

Sometimes I still catch myself imagining how wonderful we could have been together, but I have to accept that fate has written a different story for us.

Thank you for stepping into my world and making my life so much brighter when I needed it most.

I will always cherish those memories, and a part of me will always love you.

The only difference is that starting today, my love for you will live in silence—quietly carried in my heart, rather than shown to the world.

Some stories are meant to be found.
