For the first time in my life, I fell in love unconditionally, without any clear reason other than the simple desire to have you by my side.

Ironically, our paths had crossed long before we ever officially met, completely unaware of who we were to each other.

As time moved forward, it slowly brought us closer, and within a few months, we finally got to know one another.

At first, there was nothing extraordinary between us. But as the days passed, I found myself becoming more and more drawn to you.

For three months, I quietly watched from afar, collecting small and beautiful moments that I never imagined would one day become some of my most treasured memories.

Eventually, I gathered the courage to tell you how I felt.

Things didn't unfold the way I had hoped they would, but somehow we kept holding on and continued to stay in each other's lives.

As the months passed, we experienced so much together.

There were beautiful moments that I will carry with me forever, but there were difficult ones too.

Small cracks began to appear between us, and although we always tried to mend them, I eventually found myself growing tired of asking my heart the same question over and over again:

"What are we, and where do we stand?"

So when July finally arrived, I made the hardest decision I have ever had to make.

Not because I wanted to leave.

Not because I hated you.

And certainly not because I stopped caring.

I simply could not continue living in a place where my heart was constantly searching for answers that never seemed to come.

Perhaps this is one of those bittersweet lessons life gives us—to remind us how precious someone can become, even if they were only ever meant to stay for a season.

Still, I will forever hold onto the beautiful routine we built throughout these past ten months.

Our first date.

Our first time going to the mall together.

The simple joy of buying snacks.

The endless conversations.

Even the frustrating moments that once felt so overwhelming.

Because in the end, none of those difficult days could ever outweigh the happiness you brought into my life.

They say that letting go is a process, but they never tell you how devastatingly difficult it is to take that final step.

Even now, when the world grows quiet, I still catch myself drifting back through the hallways of our past, wondering how beautiful our story might have been if life had simply allowed us a little more time.

But reality always finds its way back to me, gently reminding me that fate has written a different ending for us.

One where our paths must eventually diverge.

Looking back, I can still hear the echoes of everything we used to be.

Your laughter.

The late-night conversations that felt like secrets only we understood.

The silent moments that somehow said more than words ever could.

And the quiet comfort of simply knowing you were there.

You stepped into my life at a time when I was searching for solid ground, and somehow you made everything feel lighter.

For that, I will always be grateful.

I promise I will keep those memories safe.

Hidden away in the quietest corner of my heart, untouched by the passing of time.

And as strange as it may sound, I think a part of me will always love you.

No matter where life takes us.

No matter who we become.

No matter how much time passes.

That feeling may never truly disappear.

The only thing that must change is the way I carry it.

From this day forward, my love for you will live quietly in silence.

Not as a love that asks to be seen.

Not as a love that asks for anything in return.

But simply as something beautiful that once existed.

Something I was lucky enough to experience.

And so I leave it here, among the pages of our past, wishing you nothing but happiness and beautiful chapters ahead.

Dont be sad or whatever, i just want you to be happy and i hope you will always remember me as a good person in your life.
